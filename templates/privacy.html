Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

At Fortify, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our API, online portal, and mobile application. This policy is designed to comply with the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 under the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000 ("IT Act").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal Identification Information: When you register for our services, we collect personal information, such as your email address. You may also provide other personal details if necessary for account management.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you use the API, such as the IP address, API requests, and URLs scanned. This helps us provide a better service, improve our security features, and protect against abuse.
  • Device and Connection Information: Information about the device you are using, such as your browser type, operating system, and device identifier.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide our services: To allow you to access and use our API, portal, and mobile application.
  • For security and abuse protection: We collect your email address and IP address to help detect, prevent, and respond to abuse.
  • To improve our service: We use usage data to analyze trends and enhance our products.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect non-personal information. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but please note that disabling cookies may affect functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption and access controls.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Fortify Support.
