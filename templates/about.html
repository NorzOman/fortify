About Fortify

A student-driven initiative to make security scanning accessible, private, and accurate

Our Mission

Born as a college project, Fortify aims to democratize security scanning by providing a free, privacy-focused alternative to commercial solutions. We believe that robust security tools should be accessible to everyone, from individual developers to small businesses.

Privacy First

Unlike traditional antivirus solutions like Avast, we don't store or sell your data. Your scans remain completely private and are processed in real-time without persistent storage.

Curated Database

While we respect VirusTotal as the industry standard, we take a different approach. Our carefully curated database focuses on minimizing false positives, giving you more accurate and actionable results.

Free & Open

As a student project, we're committed to keeping Fortify free and accessible. We believe in transparency and continuous improvement through community feedback.

Focused Scope

While we may not match the breadth of VirusTotal's multi-vendor approach, our specialized focus allows us to excel in specific security domains while maintaining high accuracy.

A Student Initiative

Fortify started as a college project with a simple goal: to make security scanning more accessible and privacy-focused. While we acknowledge industry leaders like VirusTotal as our inspiration, we're carving our own path by prioritizing user privacy and scan accuracy.
